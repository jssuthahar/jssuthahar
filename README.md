> *I don't just build software — I obsess over it. Every architecture decision, every line of code, every deployment pipeline is an opportunity to do something better than yesterday. After 18+ years, that drive hasn't dimmed — it's sharper.*

I'm a Cloud, AI & Mobile Solutions Architect who genuinely loves what I do. I find the hard problems interesting, not exhausting — whether that's designing a zero-trust identity model, squeezing 20% out of cloud spend with FinOps, or figuring out why an offline-first sync engine fails at the edge. I learn constantly, I share everything I learn, and I believe the best architects are the ones still writing code.

Currently leading cloud and mobile architecture for a global manufacturing enterprise across **54 markets in EMEA, APAC, and the US**. Delivering at scale means nothing breaks quietly — so everything I build is observable, governed, and built to last.

## 🔥 What drives me

- **Architecture that actually ships** — I care about elegant design, but I care more about solutions that go live, perform under load, and are maintainable a year later by someone who wasn't in the room
- **Staying current, on purpose** — the field moves fast; I make time every week to read, experiment, and rebuild my mental models. What I learn goes straight onto [msdevbuild.com](https://www.msdevbuild.com)
- **Teaching as learning** — I've published 200+ technical articles and maintain two YouTube channels because explaining something forces you to truly understand it
- **Code as craft** — 18 years in and I still write code. Not because I have to, but because I believe architects who stop coding lose their instincts
